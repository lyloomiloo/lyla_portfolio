# Task: Place retro elements throughout the page
Plan: `/_plans/07-design-refinements.md` → Step 2.2
Spec: `/_specs/07-design-refinements.md`

## Context
The retro elements exist as components — now scatter them throughout the page for personality.

## What to build
Insert RetroElements at strategic positions in the layout.

## Technical details

### Suggested placements
1. **Marquee ticker** — between hero and projects section. Full width.
2. **Pixel art character** — positioned near the hero, below the description text or near the green frame area.
3. **REC indicator** — top-right area of the about section (near the signal log).
4. **Loading bar** — between projects and about section, or inside the about section.
5. **ASCII divider** — between about and skills sections, and/or between skills and contact.
6. **Kaomoji** — near the contact section or floating near the hero.

### Integration
Import RetroElements in index.astro and place between/within sections:
```astro
<Hero />
<RetroElement type="marquee" />
<section id="projects">...</section>
<RetroElement type="ascii-divider" />
<section id="about">...</section>
<RetroElement type="loading-bar" />
<section id="skills">...</section>
<RetroElement type="ascii-divider" />
<section id="contact">...</section>
```

The pixel character and REC indicator can be placed inside their respective sections with absolute positioning.

## Files to create/modify
- `src/pages/index.astro` — add RetroElement components at key positions

## Acceptance criteria
- At least 4 retro elements visible on the page
- Elements feel organic, not forced
- Responsive — simplified on mobile
