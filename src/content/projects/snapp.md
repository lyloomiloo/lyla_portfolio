## The Insight

We walk past a thousand interesting things every day without seeing them.

## How It Works

A mobile web app that gives everyone in Barcelona the same single-word prompt each day — *texture*, *tiny*, or *bright*. Step outside, find it, snap a photo, drop it on a shared city map. At midnight, the map resets.

No likes, no followers, no algorithm. Just a quiet dare to look more carefully.

## The Reframing

A cracked tile becomes the answer to *pattern*. A stranger's dog becomes *soft*. The shared map turns Barcelona into a collaborative, ever-changing gallery that lives for exactly one day.

## What I Built

Research & concept. Full Figma prototypes. Next.js frontend. Supabase backend. Leaflet mapping. Deployed on Vercel, currently in beta.

## Design Philosophy

Deliberately simple. No gamification layers, no streaks, no social pressure. The constraint of one word and one day creates focus. The shared map creates connection. Anti-social media — using a phone to make you look up from it.
