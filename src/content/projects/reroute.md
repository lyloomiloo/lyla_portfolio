## The Question

Every navigation app solves the same problem: getting you there fast. (re)Route solves a different one — making the walk worth taking.

## How It Works

You open the app. You type what you actually want: "calm 20 min walk," "artsy date in Born," "take me to Park Güell but make it scenic," or just "surprise me." A single AI call interprets your words — not as a search query, but as an intent — and builds a walk around it.

### Three Entry Points, Three Mindsets

**GO** — destination search with route options that explain *why* each route is different, not just how long it takes.

**EXPLORE** — type a vibe, the AI curates an experience. From a themed architecture trail to a multi-stop evening out.

**WANDER** — a compass, curated pins around you, and nothing else. No route. No destination. Just direction.

## The Data Underneath

119,000 Barcelona street segments scored across noise, greenery, cleanliness, cultural richness, and safety. When you ask for "calm," the system knows which streets are actually quiet. After dark, every route automatically shifts to well-lit, active streets. Safety isn't a mode. It's a baseline.

## Smart Discovery

Ask for "artsy date" and the system reasons about what the query implies — experimental galleries, design bookshops, natural wine bars with art on the walls — then verifies every suggestion against real place data before showing it.

## Curated Layer

386 hand-curated points of interest. Themed walks spanning Gaudí, Gothic Barcelona, modernisme, hidden courtyards, public art, rooftop views, Roman ruins. A bloom calendar tracks 8 flowering species by month.

## The Research

8 in-depth interviews. 89% of survey respondents already vary their routes to explore. The tools just haven't caught up.

## What This Is

A master's thesis prototype — working proof that navigation can be designed around experience, not just efficiency. Built for Barcelona. Designed for any city worth walking.
